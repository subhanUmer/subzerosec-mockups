Threat Intelligence Analyst

Threat Intelligence &middot; Mid &middot; Remote (UK/EU) &middot; Full-time

Turn raw adversary behavior into the scenarios our red team runs and the hypotheses our hunters chase across client environments.

What you will do

  • Track threat groups and campaigns relevant to the sectors we serve
  • Translate adversary tradecraft into red-team scenarios and hunting hypotheses
  • Produce intelligence assessments that inform engagement scoping
  • Maintain mappings of relevant techniques to MITRE ATT&CK
  • Support hunts and operations with timely, actionable intelligence

What we are looking for

  • Experience in cyber threat intelligence or a closely related discipline
  • Strong grasp of adversary tactics, techniques, and the ATT&CK framework
  • Ability to turn ambiguous data into clear, decision-ready assessments
  • Excellent written communication for technical and executive audiences
  • Analytical rigor and structured-analysis discipline

Nice to have

  • Sector specialization in finance, healthcare, or telecom threats
  • Hands-on detection engineering or hunting experience
