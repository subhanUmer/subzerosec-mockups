# PRODUCT.md — SubZeroSec

## Register
Brand / marketing site. Landing-page-shaped, brand-forward, conversion-oriented. Not an app.

## Product purpose
Marketing site for **SubZeroSec**, an offensive-security firm. It explains five services
(Penetration Testing/VAPT, Red Teaming, Threat Hunting, OSINT & Exposure, Attack Surface
Analysis), builds credibility through case studies, trust/compliance, and disclosed-research
proof, and converts visitors into **booked security assessments**.

Tagline: **"Before Zero. Beyond Breach."** — intercept vulnerabilities on the path to
becoming zero-days, before a breach happens.

## Target users
- **Security leaders** (CISO, Director of Security Operations) evaluating an offensive-security partner.
- **Technical evaluators** (security engineers) who judge depth and rigor, not marketing.
- **Procurement / compliance** confirming standards (CREST, ISO 27001, SOC 2, PCI DSS, CHECK).

Primary conversion: "Book an assessment" / "Book a free consultation".

## Brand personality
Precise, technical, confident, adversary-minded. Proof over promises. Calm authority, not
fear-mongering. Reads like operators who compromise systems for a living — because they do.

## Anti-references (what to avoid)
- Generic SaaS sites with purple/blue AI gradients and three identical feature cards.
- Fear-mongering security vendors (red alarms, padlock clichés, breach-countdown theatrics).
- "Scanner reseller" energy — automated-tool dashboards presented as expertise.

## Strategic design principles
1. **Proof, not promises.** Lead with evidence (working exploits, anonymized case studies, disclosed research).
2. **Technical restraint.** Dark, sparse, legible. One blue accent, no decoration for its own sake.
3. **Adversary's-eye framing.** Copy and structure show the org the way an attacker sees it.
4. **Legibility first.** Dense security content stays scannable; numerics in mono for precision.
