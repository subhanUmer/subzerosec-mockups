Security Consultant (Delivery)

Client Delivery &middot; Mid &middot; Remote (UK/EU) &middot; Full-time

Own the client experience from scoping to verified remediation, making sure technical rigor lands as clear, actionable outcomes.

What you will do

  • Scope engagements with clients and translate needs into clear statements of work
  • Coordinate delivery across testing, red-team, and intelligence teams
  • Guide clients through findings, prioritization, and remediation planning
  • Track remediation to closure and coordinate retests
  • Act as the trusted point of contact throughout the engagement lifecycle

What we are looking for

  • Experience in security consulting, delivery, or technical program management
  • Enough technical depth to discuss findings credibly with engineers
  • Excellent communication and stakeholder-management skills
  • Strong organization across concurrent engagements
  • A consultative, outcome-focused approach to client relationships

Nice to have

  • Background in a regulated sector such as finance or healthcare
  • Familiarity with SOC 2, ISO 27001, or PCI DSS requirements
