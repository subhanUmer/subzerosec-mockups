Red Team Operator

Adversary Emulation &middot; Mid–Senior &middot; Remote (UK/EU) &middot; Full-time

Run objective-based adversary emulation against mature, defended environments and measure how well clients can detect and respond.

What you will do

  • Plan and execute covert, objective-based red-team operations
  • Develop initial access, escalation, lateral movement, and persistence under detection thresholds
  • Map every action to MITRE ATT&CK and document detection gaps and response timing
  • Run purple-team replays to help blue teams build durable detections
  • Build and maintain tradecraft, infrastructure, and tooling that evades modern defenses

What we are looking for

  • Demonstrable red-team or advanced penetration testing experience
  • Strong Active Directory and Windows internals knowledge
  • Familiarity with C2 frameworks, evasion, and operational security
  • Working fluency with the MITRE ATT&CK framework
  • Sound judgment operating quietly in live production environments

Nice to have

  • Malware development or evasion research background
  • Social engineering or physical intrusion experience
