Senior Penetration Tester

Offensive Security &middot; Senior &middot; Remote (UK/EU) &middot; Full-time

Lead manual, exploit-driven assessments across web, API, network, and cloud targets, and set the technical bar for the findings we ship.

What you will do

  • Plan and deliver penetration tests across web applications, APIs, networks, and cloud
  • Manually exploit and chain vulnerabilities to prove real business impact
  • Write clear, prioritized reports that engineers can act on and leaders can understand
  • Brief clients on findings and support them through remediation and retest
  • Mentor junior testers and contribute to internal tooling and methodology

What we are looking for

  • Several years of hands-on penetration testing across multiple target types
  • Deep, practical knowledge of web and API exploitation beyond automated tooling
  • Comfort operating in at least one major cloud provider
  • Strong written communication — your reports are the product
  • A relevant certification such as OSCP, CREST, or equivalent demonstrable skill

Nice to have

  • Cloud or mobile security specialization
  • Published research, tooling, or conference talks
