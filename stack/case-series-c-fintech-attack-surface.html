The challenge

The company had scaled from one product to a platform in two years, and its asset inventory had not kept pace. Ahead of an enterprise sales push, it needed to know what it was actually exposing to the internet.

Our approach

  • Discovered and attributed the full external footprint, including forgotten and shadow assets
  • Mined public repositories and breach corpora for leaked credentials and secrets
  • Identified exposed development environments and over-privileged cloud identities
  • Set up continuous monitoring so new exposure surfaces as the estate changes
